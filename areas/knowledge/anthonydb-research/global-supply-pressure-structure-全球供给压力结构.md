## Chinese Summary / 中文摘要

### 保留原因

- 这不是一篇短结论稿，而是一篇带数据骨架的方法论笔记。
- 它把“结构性供给压力”从传统工业过剩扩展到数字内容、软件/AI 产品、平台商家与资产供给等更广的范围。

### 核心结论

1. 结构性供给压力不只是“产能太多”，而是供给持续扩张与系统真实吸收能力之间的长期错配。
2. 吸收能力要同时看家庭消费、信用条件、注意力/时间和出口承接，而不只是单一需求指标。
3. 国家层面的对比仍然有用：中国整体压力更高，美国整体中等但数字内容与 AI 软件供给压力明显，日本和越南各有各的结构性脆弱点。
4. 房地产是最典型的存量型过剩行业，一旦人口、信用和需求吸收走弱，调整周期会非常长。
5. 这篇的长期价值很大程度上来自方法：联合国国民账户、官方统计、BIS/FRED 信号、IMF 报告和数字行业 proxy 被放进了同一套框架。
6. 一个耐用的宏观结论是，结构性供给压力通常通过价格压缩、补贴、出口外部化和财政金融吸收来出清。

## Retained takeaways

1. Structural supply pressure is broader than classic industrial overcapacity.
   The historical note extends it to:
   - digital content and ad inventory
   - software / AI product supply
   - platform merchant and SKU proliferation
   - financial leverage and asset oversupply

2. The important unit is not just "too much production."
   It is persistent mismatch between expanding supply and the system's real absorption capacity:
   - household consumption
   - credit conditions
   - attention and time
   - export absorption

3. The note's country-level picture is still useful:
   - China: high structural pressure across manufacturing, real estate, platform commerce
   - US: medium overall, but notable pressure in digital content, AI/software product supply, and some commercial real estate
   - Japan: medium, with aging-linked pressure in real estate and education
   - Vietnam: medium, especially where manufacturing and logistics depend on external demand

4. The note treats real estate as the canonical stock-based oversupply sector.
   Once population, credit, and demand absorption weaken, the adjustment can last for years.

5. The note's methodological value is high.
   It combines:
   - UN national accounts
   - official country statistics
   - BIS/FRED credit signals
   - IMF country reporting
   - proxy metrics for digital and attention-driven sectors

6. A durable macro insight from the note:
   structural supply pressure is often resolved through
   - price compression / subsidy
   - export externalization
   - fiscal-financial absorption
