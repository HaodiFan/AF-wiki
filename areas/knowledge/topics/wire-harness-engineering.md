# Wire Harness Engineering / 线束工程

## Definition

Wire harness engineering is the systems discipline for designing, routing, manufacturing, validating, and maintaining bundled electrical interconnects across vehicles, aircraft, industrial equipment, and other complex products.

The durable engineering lens is not just "wires that connect things"; it is a lifecycle system spanning logical connectivity, topology, 3D routing, formboard/manufacturing data, reliability, standards, traceability, and serviceability.

## Why it matters

- It exposes the physical cost of product complexity: weight, package space, connectors, variants, manufacturing labor, and change management.
- It sits at the boundary of electrical, mechanical, manufacturing, quality, and lifecycle engineering.
- It is a useful non-software reference case for data-driven engineering, digital thread, manufacturability, verification, and automation limits.
