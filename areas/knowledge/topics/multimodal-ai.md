# Multimodal AI / 多模态 AI

## Definition

Multimodal AI covers models and agents that reason over text, images, video, vision-language grounding, spatial-temporal scenes, and other non-text signals.

In this wiki, the topic is connected to agent systems when perception becomes part of action, tool use, or evidence-grounded reasoning.

## Why it matters

- Multimodal perception is a key path toward agentic vision and real-world interaction.
- It connects evaluation, grounding, data composition, and tool use.
- It creates additional safety and traceability requirements for evidence-based agent behavior.
