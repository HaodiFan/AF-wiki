# Quantum Computing / 量子计算

## Definition

Quantum computing studies computation based on quantum states, circuits, measurement, noise, and algorithms. Quantum machine learning studies intersections between quantum computation and ML methods.

In this wiki, this is currently a source-document topic rather than an active curated research map.

## Why it matters

- It preserves a separate technical track from agent systems and LLMs.
- It may later connect to scientific computing, AI4Science, or specialized hardware research.
- Several source documents in BaiduSync are quantum-related and should remain discoverable inside the wiki.
