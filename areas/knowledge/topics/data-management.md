# Data Management / 数据管理

## Definition

Data management covers data governance, compliance, privacy, data trading, data platforms, and organizational practices for making data usable, legal, traceable, and economically valuable.

For agent and AI systems, this topic is part of the infrastructure layer: agents depend on usable data, permission boundaries, provenance, and compliance-aware workflows.

## Why it matters

- AI systems are constrained by data availability, quality, compliance, and provenance.
- Data governance determines what personal, enterprise, and cross-border data an agent can use.
- Data markets and data-product thinking affect AI application strategy and business model design.
