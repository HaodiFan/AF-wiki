## Main rationale
- Abdominal fat cannot be spot-reduced by ab exercises alone
- Main fat-loss drivers are sustained calorie deficit and higher whole-body training/activity volume
- Core work is useful for abdominal strength and appearance, but not the primary mechanism for reducing belly fat
- User dislikes dedicated leg day, so lower-body work is distributed in small amounts across the week instead of assigning a full leg day

## Nutrition assumption in this version
- Energy intake target: ~1900-2050 kcal/day with a mild deficit

## Weekly structure
### Monday — Chest + triceps + core
- Flat barbell or dumbbell bench press: 4 x 8-10
- Incline dumbbell bench press: 3 x 10-12
- Cable fly: 3 x 12-15
- Cable pressdown: 3 x 12
- Lying triceps extension: 3 x 10-12
- Hanging leg raise: 3 x 10-15
- Plank: 3 x 45-60 sec

### Tuesday — Swimming
- 40-60 min, mainly freestyle
- Can mix in breaststroke
- Stretch shoulders and hips after

### Wednesday — Back + biceps + lower-body insert
- Pull-up or lat pulldown: 4 x 8-10
- Seated cable row: 3 x 10-12
- One-arm dumbbell row: 3 x 10
- Barbell curl: 3 x 10-12
- Hammer curl: 3 x 12
- Goblet squat: 3 x 12
- Kettlebell deadlift: 3 x 12

### Thursday — Shoulders + arms + core
- Dumbbell shoulder press: 4 x 8-10
- Lateral raise: 4 x 15-20
- Reverse fly or face pull: 3 x 15
- One extra biceps movement: 3 x 12
- One extra triceps movement: 3 x 12
- Crunch: 3 x 15-20
- Russian twist: 3 x 20
- Dead bug: 3 x 10 each side

### Friday — Full-body metabolic training + core
- Kettlebell swing: 4 x 15
- Bulgarian split squat: 3 x 10 each side
- Push-up: 3 x near failure
- Battle rope: 4 x 30 sec
- Farmer's walk: 3 x 40 m
- Hanging leg raise: 3 x 12
- Ab roller: 3 x 10

### Saturday — Swimming
- 40-60 min

### Sunday — Easy recovery swim
- 30-40 min, easy pace

## Structural notes
- Core work is split across Monday, Thursday, and Friday, around 10 minutes each time
- Lower-body work is inserted on Wednesday and Friday rather than using a full dedicated leg day
- Friday is positioned as the main high-heart-rate / fat-loss-support session
- Swim frequency in this version is 3x per week: Tue, Sat, Sun
