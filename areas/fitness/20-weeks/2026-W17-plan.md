# Next Week Fitness Plan Draft

> Week of 2026-04-20
> Basis: recent history in W16, current nutrition targets, and current mixed swimming + resistance pattern.

## Context used
- Last week included swimming on Wednesday and Saturday.
- Last resistance session logged was Thursday: shoulders + biceps + triceps.
- No recent lower-body day logged.
- No recent chest-focused or back-focused resistance day logged.
- Current nutrition target is ~2000-2050 kcal and ~156g protein/day.

## Main planning intent for next week
- Keep swimming in the week because it fits current preference/history.
- Avoid over-repeating shoulder/arm emphasis immediately.
- Fill the current coverage gaps with lower body, push, and pull work.
- Use a moderate 4-session structure plus 1 optional recovery/mobility day.

## Plan

### Day 1 — Lower body + core
- Goblet squat or barbell squat: 4 x 6-10
- Romanian deadlift: 4 x 8-10
- Walking lunges: 3 x 10-12 each side
- Leg curl or hip hinge accessory: 3 x 10-15
- Standing calf raise: 3 x 12-15
- Plank: 3 rounds
- Optional finish: incline walk 10-15 min

### Day 2 — Swim / aerobic recovery
- Swimming 30-45 min at moderate pace
- Keep this mostly aerobic, not all-out intervals
- 5-10 min shoulder and hip mobility after

### Day 3 — Push (chest + shoulders + triceps, moderate shoulder volume)
- Flat dumbbell or barbell press: 4 x 6-10
- Incline dumbbell press: 3 x 8-12
- Machine chest press or push-ups: 3 x 10-15
- Lateral raise: 2-3 x 12-15
- Triceps pressdown or overhead extension: 3 x 10-15
- Optional: easy cardio 10 min

### Day 4 — Rest / mobility / walking
- 7000-10000 steps
- Light stretch or mobility 10-15 min
- If fatigue is high, make this a full rest day

### Day 5 — Pull (back + biceps)
- Lat pulldown or pull-ups: 4 x 8-12
- Chest-supported row or seated cable row: 4 x 8-12
- One-arm dumbbell row: 3 x 10-12 each side
- Rear delt fly: 2-3 x 12-15
- Dumbbell curl or cable curl: 3 x 10-15
- Optional finish: zone-2 cardio 10 min

### Day 6 — Swim or conditioning
- Option A: swimming 30-40 min easy-moderate
- Option B: bike / incline walk 25-35 min if you do not want to swim
- Keep intensity controlled unless recovery feels excellent

### Day 7 — Optional full-body light pump or full rest
If energy and schedule are good:
- Leg press or squat variation: 3 x 10
- Press variation: 3 x 10
- Row variation: 3 x 10
- Curls / triceps superset: 2 x 12
- Core 5-8 min

If tired:
- Full rest

## Intensity guidance
- Most sets should stop with 1-3 reps in reserve.
- Do not chase failure on every movement.
- Because last week already had shoulder/arm work plus swimming, treat this week as a rebalance week, not an ego week.

## Nutrition application for this week
- Keep protein near ~156g/day.
- Use the existing dinner template as the default anchor.
- On swim / rest days, carbs can sit at the lower end of your range.
- On lower body and pull days, keep lunch and dinner carbs more complete.
- If fats run high, use beef shank instead of salmon at dinner.

## Grocery logic
This plan assumes:
- 4-5 structured breakfasts
- 5-7 high-protein lunches/dinners
- repeated use of your current dinner and pre-sleep template
