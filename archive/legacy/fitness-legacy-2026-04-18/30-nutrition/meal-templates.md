# Meal Templates

## Training day dinner template
- Rice (120-150g cooked)
- Broccoli
- Bell pepper
- Salmon 200g or beef shank 200g

## Lower-fat correction dinner
- Rice (small bowl)
- Broccoli
- Bell pepper
- Sliced beef shank instead of salmon

## Pre-sleep protein correction
- Unsweetened Greek yogurt 200g
- 2 boiled eggs
- 5-6 almonds
