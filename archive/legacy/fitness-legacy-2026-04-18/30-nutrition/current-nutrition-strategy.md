# Nutrition Strategy

## Current active targets
- Calories: ~2000-2050 kcal/day
- Protein: ~156g/day target
- Fat: ~55-65g/day preferred

## Practical rules
- Use a high-protein dinner main to complete the day
- Prefer salmon 200g or beef shank 200g for dinner protein
- Use Greek yogurt + eggs as the default pre-sleep protein correction
- Reduce dinner rice if lunch and drinks already pushed carbs high

## Notes
- If fat is trending too high, prefer beef shank over salmon
